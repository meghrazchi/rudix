# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration in all interactions:

- Use constructive, technical feedback.
- Assume positive intent while reviewing work.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Keep discussions inclusive and free of harassment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration workflows
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers through your team's internal channel or private maintainer contact process.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in removal of collaboration access.
